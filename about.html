About Us

Welcome to Ankita Dental Clinic, your trusted dental care partner in Pawas. We are committed to providing high-quality dental services that are accessible, affordable, and centered on patient satisfaction. Our team of experienced dental professionals is dedicated to delivering compassionate care and creating beautiful, healthy smiles for our community.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, quality dental services to our community with a focus on patient satisfaction, comfort, and oral health. We believe in treating every patient as family and ensuring their complete dental wellness and confidence in their smile.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and accessible dental clinic in Pawas and surrounding areas, known for our excellence, advanced techniques, and patient-centered approach. We aim to make quality dental care affordable for everyone.

Our Team

Our team consists of highly qualified dental professionals including experienced dentists, hygienists, and support staff who are committed to providing the best dental care possible. We regularly update our skills through continuing education and stay current with the latest advancements in dentistry.

Our Commitment

We use state-of-the-art equipment and follow strict sterilization protocols to ensure patient safety. Every patient receives personalized treatment plans tailored to their specific needs and goals. We believe in educating our patients about oral health and preventive care.

Our Facilities

Ankita Dental Clinic is equipped with modern dental technology including digital X-rays, intraoral cameras, and high-speed dental drills for precise and efficient treatment. Our operatory is designed with patient comfort in mind, featuring comfortable recliners, ambient lighting, and soothing music to create a relaxing environment.

Quality & Safety Standards

We strictly adhere to international standards of sterilization and infection control. All instruments are autoclaved before each use, and we maintain rigorous hygiene protocols throughout the clinic. Patient safety is our top priority in every procedure we perform.

Continuing Education

Dr. Ankita regularly participates in continuing dental education programs and workshops to stay updated with the latest advancements in dentistry. This commitment to lifelong learning ensures that our patients receive the most current and effective treatment options available.
