Our Dental Services

Complete Dental Care Solutions for Your Family

Comprehensive Dental Care

From preventive care to advanced restorative treatments, we offer a complete range of dental services to meet all your oral health needs.

Preventive Checkup

Regular dental examination and oral hygiene assessment. Early detection of cavities, gum disease, and other dental problems to maintain optimal oral health.

Professional Cleaning

Deep cleaning and scaling to remove tartar and plaque buildup. Keeps your teeth healthy, white, and free from bacteria and disease-causing organisms.

Cavity Filling

Quick and painless treatment using tooth-colored composite materials. Restores tooth function and prevents further decay and damage.

Root Canal Treatment

Expert endodontic care for infected or severely damaged teeth. Relieves pain and saves your natural tooth from extraction.

Tooth Extraction

Safe and gentle extraction with minimal discomfort. Includes proper aftercare instructions and advice on replacement options.

Orthodontics (Braces)

Braces and aligners for correcting misaligned teeth and bite problems. Achieve a perfectly aligned, beautiful smile you'll love.

Teeth Whitening

Professional teeth whitening treatment for a brighter, whiter smile. Safe and effective methods for lasting results.

Dental Implants

Permanent solution for missing teeth. Looks and functions like natural teeth, restoring your confidence and oral functionality.

Why Regular Dental Care Matters

Your oral health is a crucial part of your overall health. Regular dental care helps prevent serious dental diseases, maintains a beautiful smile, and contributes to your general well-being. At Ankita Dental Clinic, we focus on:

  • ✓ Prevention: Regular checkups and cleanings to prevent cavities, gum disease, and other oral health issues
  • ✓ Early Detection: Identifying problems early when they're easier and less expensive to treat
  • ✓ Patient Education: Teaching you proper oral hygiene techniques and habits for long-term dental health
  • ✓ Quality Treatment: Using latest techniques and materials for the best results

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting your dentist at least twice a year for regular checkups and cleanings. However, patients with specific conditions may need more frequent visits.

Is dental treatment painful?

Modern anesthesia and our gentle techniques make dental treatment comfortable and virtually pain-free. We prioritize your comfort in every procedure.
